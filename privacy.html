Privacy Policy

Last updated: 6 June 2026

Registo Bizarro (“we”, “us”) operates the channel @registobizarro on YouTube, TikTok and Instagram. This software application is used solely by us to publish our own original short videos to our own social-media accounts through the platforms’ official APIs.

What the app accesses

The app uses the TikTok Content Posting API only to upload our own videos to our own account (drafts/inbox). It authenticates through TikTok’s OAuth and stores the resulting access and refresh tokens securely and privately on our own computer. Tokens are used only to upload content to our account.

What we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect, store, process, or share the personal data of any other person or user.
  • We do not access followers, direct messages, comments, or analytics belonging to third parties.
  • We do not handle any user-generated content other than our own uploads.
  • We do not use cookies, trackers, or advertising identifiers.

Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or transfer any data to third parties. Access tokens are never shared and are stored only on our own device.

Data retention & deletion

The only data stored are our own account’s OAuth tokens, kept locally for as long as the integration is in use. They can be revoked at any time from the account’s connected-apps settings, after which they are deleted.

Changes

We may update this policy; any changes will be posted on this page with a new “last updated” date.
